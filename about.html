About TUA Academy

TUA Academy is a math channel and resource hub focused on the TMSCA General Math test. I (Guhan) create short, high-impact videos that focus on problem types, timing strategies, and common mistakes.

My Story

I spent two years preparing for the Middle School State Championship test. When the results weren't what I hoped for I was upset for a while. Eventually I realized that the knowledge I'd gained could help other students — if I taught what I learned. Turning that failure into a success is how TUA Academy began.

What I provide

  • Video lessons and clear walkthroughs for TMSCA topics
  • Practice tests and formula/reference sheets
  • Concise problem-solving tips for test day
